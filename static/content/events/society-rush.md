# **Society Rush**


On September 13, the "Biggest Block Party of the Year" commenced with
societies competing for the attention and affection of the incoming
class. With enticement of delicious food, rowdy games, decorated tents,
and loud cheers, society members tried to attract new students to their
society. The following night, at Induction, students officially declared
their choice. This Society Rush tradition allows students to welcome
newcomers to the distinctive traditions of each society. Here they will
be able to foster friendships, develop leadership skills, and encourage
one another in Christ.
