# Instrumental Groups


A broad array of BJU instrumental groups open avenues for and cultivate
students' musical talents. These groups include the University Symphony
Orchestra and Symphonic Wind Band, as well as ensembles for handbell,
harp, trumpet, tuba-euphonium, trombone, flute, saxophone, and horn.
Students rehearse hours in preparation for concerts, which often feature
both religious and classical pieces. In October ensembles participated
in the High School Festival "Kaleidoscope Concert." Numerous groups took
their expertise to local churches, enhancing students' outreach ministry
in service to God.
