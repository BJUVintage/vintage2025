# Turkey Bowl

The 2024 Turkey Bowl featured an intramural soccer match between the
Sigma Alpha Chi Spartans and the Pi Gamma Delta Royals. The hard-fought
game demonstrated skill and sportsmanship with the Royals winning the
championship. Before the Turkey Bowl commenced, the Nu Alpha Phi Flames
competed against the Theta Sigma Chi Colts in another soccer match
called the Turkey Cup, with the Flames securing the victory. Spectators
at the Turkey Bowl also enjoyed the concession stand's offerings and
events hosted by the Inter-Society Council (ISC), including halftime
activities.
