# Instrumental Groups

Our instrumental music ensembles showcase student talent across a variety of musical traditions and styles throughout the academic year. From the symphony orchestra to jazz band, percussion ensemble, and chamber groups, these dedicated musicians devote countless hours to rehearsals and performances. Students from all majors participate, finding creative expression, community, and the joy of collaborative music-making while entertaining audiences both on campus and during regional tours.
