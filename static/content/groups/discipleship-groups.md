# Discipleship Groups

Our student-led discipleship groups provide safe spaces for spiritual growth, meaningful conversation, and authentic community. Meeting weekly across campus, these small groups foster deep relationships centered around faith exploration, scripture study, and mutual encouragement. Students develop leadership skills while creating environments where participants can ask questions, share struggles, and celebrate growth together on their spiritual journeys.
