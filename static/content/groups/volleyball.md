# Volleyball


The Bruins volleyball team secured the South Region Championship,
defeating both Trinity Baptist College and Pensacola Christian College
3-0. The team ended its season as the National Runner-Up with a 30-12
season record. Faith Blalock, Faith Goodwin, Emily Lewis, and Anne Lloyd
earned All-Region Honors. Bethany Estell, Madison Goldstrom, Anne Lloyd,
Eden Swihart, and Emily Lewis were named Scholar-Athletes. Faith Blalock
and Hannah Goodwin also earned All-American Honors, and Coach Vickie
Denny was named South Region Coach of the Year for the fourth time in
her career.
