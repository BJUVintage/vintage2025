**CSC & CGO**

The Community Service Council (CSC) promotes ministry opportunities in
Greenville. This year, the student-led organization connected students
with civic projects such as cleaning up local parks, contributing to
food banks, donating blood, and fundraising for local non-profits. The
CSC also aids societies in organizing service outreaches, which enable
the BJU family to share the love of Christ in the community. This fall,
the CSC partnered with ARCH Ministries to provide relief from Hurricane
Helene in the Upstate and North Carolina.

The Center for Global Opportunities (CGO) is a connection hub for
students and their organizations interested in ministry opportunities
such as urban evangelism and children's Bible clubs. This year, the CGO
sponsored REACH Week. REACH is an acronym for (1) Renew my commitment to
the Gospel, (2) Emphasize the power of the Gospel, (3) Act of my belief
in the Gospel, (4) Care for people who need the Gospel, and (5) Help
ministries, using my career skills, to advance the Gospel. This ministry
focus included a community outreach fair, providing students with the
opportunity to connect with churches in the Upstate and with missions
organizations, as well as a summer ministry fair for students interested
in various ministries and global missions outreaches.
