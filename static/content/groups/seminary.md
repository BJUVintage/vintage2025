# **Seminary**


The BJU Seminary awards postgraduate degrees to Christian leaders who
desire to proclaim, serve, and advance the Gospel. To foster this goal,
the Seminary begins the fall semester with a weekend retreat at The
Wilds Christian Conference Center. Through this special event, students
immerse themselves in the Seminary's focus on cultivating Christlike
relationships and meditating on God's Word. Additionally, the Seminary
hosts the Stewart Custer Lecture Series in the fall and the CoRE
Conference in the spring. The CoRE (Connect, Renew, Equip) Conference,
striving to educate those on campus as well as those already engaged in
full-time ministry, provides special speakers who highlight contemporary
issues faced by local churches. BJU Seminary graduates serve as pastors,
teachers, and administrators as they spread out around the nation and
the world to minister God's Word to needy souls. Graduates also use
their skills to further ministries in their home churches.
