# **Mission Teams**

Bob Jones University sponsors mission teams to assist churches, provide
medical care, and share the Gospel. This year, the Center for Global
Opportunities organized teams of students to serve the Greenville
community each week; students shared their testimonies of God's love in
coffee shops and nursing homes. Beyond the Upstate, participants
collaborate with missionaries to plant churches and evangelize the lost.
This past summer, BJU sponsored missions teams to Japan and Cuba. This
global ministry enables students not only to complete course
requirements but also to advance the kingdom of God.
