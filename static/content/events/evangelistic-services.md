# Evangelistic Services

The campus community experienced profound spiritual renewal during the fall and spring evangelistic services, which brought together students, faculty, staff, and community members for a focused time of spiritual growth and outreach. The fall series featured evangelist Dr. Marcus Thompson, whose powerful messages on "Faith That Transforms" resonated deeply with attendees over the four-day event. Spring services were led by Pastor Rachel Williams, whose practical teachings on "Living the Gospel Daily" challenged participants to integrate faith into every aspect of life. Both series incorporated contemporary worship, personal testimonies, and opportunities for prayer and commitment. Attendance exceeded expectations, with overflow seating required in adjacent buildings. Student involvement was particularly notable, with over 200 students serving on prayer teams, in the worship ensemble, and as personal counselors. Many participants reported significant spiritual decisions made during these services, and the ripple effects continued throughout the semester through small group follow-up studies and discipleship relationships formed during these concentrated times of spiritual emphasis.
