# Chapel

Chapel services continued to be the heartbeat of campus spiritual life throughout the 2024-2025 academic year. Thrice-weekly gatherings brought the campus community together for worship, prayer, and spiritual instruction. This year's chapel theme, "Rooted & Reaching," encouraged students to deepen their spiritual foundations while actively engaging with the world around them. Notable guest speakers included bestselling author and pastor Dr. Timothy Reynolds, missionary and humanitarian aid worker Sarah Mbeki, and theology professor Dr. Elizabeth Chen. The worship program featured both traditional hymns and contemporary worship led by talented student musicians. Special chapel services marked significant moments in the Christian calendar, with particularly memorable Good Friday and Easter services. The spiritual emphasis weeks in both fall and spring semesters provided extended opportunities for reflection and renewal, with many students noting these times as pivotal in their spiritual formation during the academic year.
