# Mission Teams

Our mission teams extend our university's commitment to service beyond campus boundaries, partnering with communities locally and globally. Teams engage in diverse projects ranging from construction and education to healthcare and environmental initiatives during academic breaks. Through these immersive experiences, students develop cross-cultural competencies, leadership skills, and a deeper understanding of global challenges while making tangible differences in underserved communities.
