# Fall Fest

The annual Fall Fest transformed the campus into a celebration of autumn with activities, food, and fellowship. Organized by the Student Activities Council, this year's festival expanded to include the surrounding community, drawing record attendance of over 2,000 participants. The campus quad featured traditional activities including pumpkin carving contests, apple bobbing, and hayrides, while student organizations hosted creative booths offering games and seasonal treats. Live music from student bands provided a festive soundtrack throughout the day. The highlight of this year's event was the addition of a farmer's market featuring local vendors and artisans, strengthening town-gown relationships. Athletic competitions including the popular flag football tournament and 5K "Fall Colors Run" added friendly competition to the festivities. As darkness fell, the celebration continued with an outdoor movie screening of classic fall films selected by student vote. Many participants commented that Fall Fest has become a cherished tradition that builds community spirit while showcasing the beauty of campus during the autumn season.
