# Golf

Our golf team combines precision, focus, and strategic thinking as they represent our university on courses throughout the region. These student-athletes balance academic demands with rigorous practice schedules, perfecting their swing mechanics and course management skills. Beyond individual scores, team members develop mental fortitude, patience, and sportsmanship—qualities that serve them well both on the green and in their future careers.
