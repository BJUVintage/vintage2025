# **Evangelistic Services**


Each year, Bob Jones University begins fall and spring semesters with
evangelistic services. Required for students and open to the public,
these meetings emphasize the centrality of the Gospel to the
University's mission. At the beginning of the fall semester, evangelist
Brent Sivnksty challenged students to mortify sin and pursue godliness.
