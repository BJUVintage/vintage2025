# Cross Country and Track & Field


This year the men's cross country team claimed the National
Championship. Evan Seaman and Trenton Goldsmith earned All-American
honors, while Levi Gerbitz, Trenton Goldsmith, Evan Seaman, and Marc
Johnson were recognized as Scholar-Athletes. Head Coach Ken Roach was
named National Coach of the Year for his leadership and dedication. The
women's cross country team also had an outstanding season, finishing as
the National Runner-Up. Kristen Dodds, Joanna Lain, and Joy Hast
received All-American honors, and Joy Hast, Joanna Lain, Courtney Jones,
and Lauren Jones were named Scholar-Athletes, showcasing their
commitment to both athletic and academic success. The Bruins Track &
Field team completed in an indoor competition this season. The team
competed at the National Championship in February, and 14 athletes
earned Scholar-Athlete Honors.
