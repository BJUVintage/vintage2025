**Chapel**

Fall Chapel Theme: "Living for More: The Meaning of Life"

During the fall semester, Dr. Crockett walked students through the book
of Ecclesiastes. By considering life's most difficult and perplexing
questions, Dr. Crocket challenged students---as Ecclesiastes 12
exhorts---to "remember now thy Creator in the days of thy youth." This
series revealed how our culture has increasingly separated itself from
God to pursue that which is meaningless. The theme equipped the BJU
family with truth and tools to find the real meaning of life by focusing
upon God Himself.
