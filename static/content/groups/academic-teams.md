# **Academic Teams**

Academic teams in robotics, biology, government, and computer
programming develop innovative skills and enter competitive events and
career-focused activities outside the classroom. These programs provide
students with the opportunity to work with peers in their specific areas
of interest. Team members' collaboration on year-long projects prepares
students to participate in state and nationwide events. By stimulating
teams intellectually and relationally, these challenges develop
competency in academics and leadership.
