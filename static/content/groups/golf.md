# Golf


The Bruins golf team enjoyed a strong season, placing second in the
NCCAA South Region Championship and tied for third at the Eagle
Invitational in Cohutta, Georgia, where two Bruins finished in the top
ten out of 70 golfers. The NCCAA recognized Nicholas Smith as a
Scholar-Athlete, while Brandon Autry represented the team at the NCCAA
National Championship. The team's consistent performance highlighted
their competitive growth and set the stage for future success.
