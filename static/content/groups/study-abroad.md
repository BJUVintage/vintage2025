# **Study Abroad**


The office of BJU Study Abroad & Study USA sponsors classes to expand
students' horizons and develop cultural competence. Available to both
undergraduate and graduate students, Study Abroad teams receive academic
credit and experience specifically designed to enhance a student's
choice of study and discovery. Team members gain experience learning and
adapting to cultural diversity.
