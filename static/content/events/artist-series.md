# Artist Series

The Artist Series continued its tradition of bringing world-class performers to campus throughout the academic year. The series opened in September with a captivating performance by the Veritas String Quartet, whose rendition of Dvořák's "American" quartet received a standing ovation. October brought celebrated concert pianist Maria Chen, who performed works by Rachmaninoff and Chopin with technical brilliance. The winter season featured the internationally acclaimed vocal ensemble Cantus, who blended Renaissance motets with contemporary compositions in a program titled "Echoes Through Time." Spring semester highlights included jazz trumpeter Marcus Williams and his quintet, and the series concluded with a stunning ballet performance of "Giselle" by the National Ballet Company. Each visiting artist also conducted masterclasses and workshops with students, extending the educational impact beyond the performances themselves.
