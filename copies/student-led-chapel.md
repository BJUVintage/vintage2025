**Student-Led Chapel**

Student-led chapels provide students with the opportunity to share
personal testimonies and lead in worship. The men's student body
president or a seminary student delivers a challenge to the student body
and faculty. Held twice each semester, student-led chapels function
similarly to regular chapel services except students lead each aspect of
the chapel. These meetings provide a valuable time of reflection and
encouragement for the student body. This year's spiritual theme,
"Abundant Life," focused on the joy, peace, and purpose believers find
when they develop a relationship with God the Father through His Son,
Jesus Christ.
