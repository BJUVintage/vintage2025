# Basketball

Last year, the men's basketball team finished fifth in the nation with
21 wins and only five losses; this record marks the team's best winning
percentage in program history. The team, defeating Pensacola Christian
College with a 67-54 victory, earned the region championship title. The
National Christian College Athletic Association (NCCAA) named Connor
Barfield and Hayes Brush Scholar-Athletes and recognized Coach Burton
Uwarow as the National Coach of the Year for Division II.

Last year, the women's basketball team fell in the South Region
tournament in a heartbreaking 77-75 loss to Pensacola Christian College.
The NCCAA named Beth Clark, Hallie Ritter, and Kyle Worley as
Scholar-Athletes; Shea Dickinson and Anna Layne Wylder earned positions
on the Division II South All-Region Team; and Annie Miller, Bria
Dickinson, and Caitlyn Dickerson received region honors. The team
completed the season with a 12-15 record.
