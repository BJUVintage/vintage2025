# Campus Media

Our campus media teams capture the stories and moments that define our university experience through various platforms. From the student newspaper to the radio station and video production team, these groups provide opportunities for students to develop skills in journalism, broadcasting, film production, and digital media while keeping the campus community informed and entertained.
