**Discipleship Groups**

Discipleship groups enable students to strengthen their faith. As they
meet in small groups each week, they reflect on chapel messages, share
devotional thoughts and testimonies, and encourage each other. The
emphasis on interpersonal relationships that are centered on God's Word
allows students the opportunity to disciple and pray for one another as
well as the ability to think deeply about their spiritual walk with the
Lord. Guided by peers, students develop qualities of spiritual
leadership and cultivate Christ-centered relationships that can last a
lifetime.
