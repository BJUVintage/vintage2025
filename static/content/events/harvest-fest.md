# Harvest Fest

Now in its fifth year, the annual Harvest Festival brought a lively
spirit to campus as students and faculty embraced the season with
creativity and enthusiasm. The event featured a "treat trail" that
spanned campus, and participants collected candy as they experienced
memorable moments with faculty members. This year's festival highlighted
both the ingenuity of individuals and groups, with imaginative costumes
ranging from the Scooby-Doo mystery gang to a group of Founding Fathers.
A prize for the best costume and recognition for group ensembles added
keen competition to the event. Designed to foster stronger
faculty-student relationships, the Harvest Festival adds an engaging and
enjoyable tradition for the campus community.
