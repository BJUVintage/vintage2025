Bible Conference

The 2025 Bible Conference theme, "The Goodness of God," reminded
believers of God's unwavering faithfulness and love. Dr. Alan Benson
opened the conference on Tuesday, February 18, setting the tone for a
powerful week of preaching and worship. Seven additional
speakers---David Doran, Drew Conley, Gary Reimers, Greg Stiekes, Jon
Crocker, Mark Minnick, and Tim Potter---encouraged students, faculty,
and visitors to recognize God's goodness in every aspect of life. Each
service began with a personal testimony from a student, alumnus, or
faculty member who shared an encounter with God's goodness. On Wednesday
morning, the Student Leadership Council hosted a special prayer meeting,
uniting students in seeking God's blessing over the conference.

This year, BJU partnered with Jonathan's House Central Africa, a
gospel-centered ministry serving vulnerable children in the Central
African Republic through a school, orphanage, and medical clinic. The
University set a goal of \$100,000 to fund the construction of The Alice
Peterson House, a home for orphaned babies, toddlers, and older girls.
Through a record number of student-initiated fundraisers as well as
generous donations, the BJU community united in support of this mission.
By sitting under the preaching of God's Word and giving toward a cause
dedicated to hope and healing, those attending Bible Conference left the
week challenged, encouraged, and spiritually renewed.
