**Campus Media**

Bob Jones University offers a variety of media outlets for campus
communication. The *Vintage* staff is excited to celebrate its 91st year
of preserving memories of the academic year through photographs, copy,
and design. Each volume of the yearbook, centering on a specific theme,
documents student life, academic attainments, and faculty and staff
recognition. The book also serves as a valuable keepsake for students
and alumni.

The *Collegian*, a student news organization, covers the latest events
in university life. This publication, which is available in digital and
print formats, highlights students, faculty, and University
accomplishments. The award-winning newspaper publishes daily online and
features current events, faculty interviews, and articles designed to
engender friendly discussion and debate.
