# Baseball

Last year, the Bruins baseball team completed their best season in
program history with a 13-24 record. The National Christian College
Athletic Association (NCCAA) named Joshua Legnard, David Twiddy, and
Mackenzie Ritter to the Scholar-Athlete team, and Riley Ramsey and
Joshua Legnard earned All-American recognition. The team competed at the
NCCAA South Region Tournament in May.
